With decades of trusted service in Derbyshire, AV Cars isn't just a taxi company — it's your travel partner. Here's a curated list of the top 5 places to visit in Derby and Belper that are perfect to reach with a quick and reliable taxi ride.

1. Elvaston Castle Country Park – A Natural Escape Near Derby

  • Location: Elvaston, Derby
  • Distance from Derby city centre: Approx. 4 miles
  • Best for: Nature lovers, walkers, families

Just outside the bustling streets of Derby, you'll find a peaceful escape at Elvaston Castle Country Park. With over 321 acres of woodlands, lakes, and gardens, this park is a favourite spot for leisurely walks, photography, and picnics. The Gothic Revival castle adds a magical touch to your day out.

Why visit by taxi?

Parking can be limited on busy weekends. With AV Cars, you'll get dropped off at the perfect spot and picked up right when you're ready — no walking long distances with tired feet or waiting in car queues.

2. Kedleston Hall – Step into British Aristocracy

  • Location: Kedleston, Derbyshire
  • Distance from Derby city centre: Approx. 5 miles
  • Best for: History fans, architecture buffs, scenic photos

If you're craving culture and elegance, Kedleston Hall delivers. This National Trust property is a neoclassical masterpiece with opulent rooms, sprawling parkland, and a peaceful vibe perfect for a quiet retreat or romantic afternoon.

Why visit by taxi?

It's off the main public transport routes. Booking with AV Cars means you don't have to stress about how to get there or where to park — we'll handle it for you.

3. Derby Museum and Art Gallery – Culture in the City Centre

  • Location: The Strand, Derby
  • Distance from Belper: Approx. 9 miles
  • Best for: Art lovers, history enthusiasts, families

Right in the heart of the city, Derby Museum and Art Gallery features everything from ancient Egyptian artefacts to the famous Joseph Wright of Derby painting collection. Whether you're a local or just visiting, it's a great way to understand Derby's deep cultural roots.

Why visit by taxi?

City centre parking is expensive and busy. AV Cars drops you at the door — no stress, no walking in bad weather, and no hassle.

4. Belper River Gardens – A Scenic Riverside Break

  • Location: Belper
  • Best for: Picnics, riverside walks, families with kids

Belper River Gardens is a peaceful riverside escape right in the heart of Belper. Perfect for sunny days, it features landscaped gardens, a historic bandstand, and boat hire in summer. Whether you want a romantic stroll or somewhere to chill with your kids, this is the spot.

Why visit by taxi?

Local parking fills up quickly on sunny days. Instead, get door-to-door service with AV Cars. You enjoy the view; we'll handle the travel.

5. Strutt's North Mill – Discover Industrial Heritage

  • Location: Belper
  • Best for: History lovers, school trips, curious minds

Recognised as part of the Derwent Valley Mills World Heritage Site, Strutt's North Mill is a window into the Industrial Revolution. Learn how Belper helped shape the modern textile industry and see impressive engineering in action.

Why visit by taxi?

Especially useful for group trips, school visits, or older visitors — AV Cars offers vehicles for every need, including wheelchair-accessible taxis.
